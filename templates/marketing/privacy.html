Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At DynaRetire, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our retirement planning simulator.

Data Collection and Usage

  • No Data Storage: We do not store any of your financial or personal information. All data entered into our simulator is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after generating your results.
  • Excel Generation: When you use our simulator, the results are delivered to you as an Excel file. This file is generated on-demand and no copy is retained on our servers.
  • No Cookies: We do not use cookies or any other tracking mechanisms to monitor your usage of our site.

Data Security

While we process your inputs to generate retirement simulations:

  • All calculations are performed in real-time
  • Data is held in temporary memory only during the calculation process
  • No information is permanently stored
  • No data is shared with third parties

Your Control

Since we don't store any of your data:

  • There's no need to request data deletion
  • No personal information to export
  • No account creation required
  • Complete privacy in your retirement planning

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@dynaretire.com
