Privacy Policy

Our Commitment to Privacy

At DynaRetire, we take your privacy seriously. This policy outlines how we handle your information when you use our retirement planning simulator.

Data Processing and Storage

  • Real-Time Calculations: Data entered into our simulator is processed in real-time and discarded immediately after generating your Excel results.
  • No Data Collection: We neither collect, store your financial information.
  • No Server Logs: We maintain no logs of your financial data or calculations, ensuring complete transaction privacy.
  • No Tracking: We do not use cookies or tracking mechanisms to monitor site usage.
  • Local Processing: Saving and loading your retirement plans happens entirely within your browser; no data is sent to our servers during this process.
  • User-Controlled Storage: Export and save your plans to locations you control, such as your personal documents folder.

Data Security

We employ industry-standard security measures to safeguard your data during calculations:

  • HTTPS Encryption: All communication is secured using SSL/TLS encryption.
  • Secure Infrastructure: Our application runs on dedicated servers with regular security updates.
  • Isolated Processing: Each calculation is performed in its own secure environment.
  • Self-Contained System: Our servers process everything independently, with no external service connections.
  • Regular Audits: Security measures undergo periodic review and updates.

Privacy-First Design

  • No Account Required: Access all features without sign-ups, logins, or personal information.
  • Complete Data Control: You retain full ownership and control over your retirement plans.
  • Zero Data Retention: No server-side storage means no data to request deletion of.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about our privacy practices, please contact us at privacy@dynaretire.com
