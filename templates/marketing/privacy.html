Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

At NumberWalk, your privacy is paramount. This policy explains how we handle—and more importantly, don’t handle—your data when you use our financial independence planning simulator.

How We Process Your Data

  • Real-Time Calculations: All inputs are computed instantaneously in your browser and then discarded. We never store your financial details on our servers.
  • Local Storage Only: Saving and loading scenarios happens entirely within your device—no data is sent or saved remotely.
  • Zero Tracking: We use no cookies, no analytics, no ads—never any mechanism to monitor your activity.

Security Measures

  • Encrypted Transmission: All interactions use HTTPS (SSL/TLS) to protect in-transit data.
  • Isolated Processing: Calculations run in ephemeral, self-contained environments—nothing persists.
  • Hardened Infrastructure: Our service runs on dedicated servers with regular security updates and no third-party connections.

Privacy-First by Design

  • No Account Needed: Get full access without signing up or sharing personal information.
  • Complete Anonymity: Since we collect and retain zero user data, there’s nothing to expose or erase.
  • Open Source Transparency: Our code is public—anyone can audit how (and where) data is handled.

Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Email us anytime at privacy@numberwalk.com.
