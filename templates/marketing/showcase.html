The Brief Scenario: Martin's Early Retirement Plan

Current Age: 45 Target Retirement: 50

Martin is 45 and wants to plan for early retirement. Here's his detailed retirement scenario:

  • He and his employer continue to contribute to the pension fund, and Martin also saves extra in his investments specifically for retirement
  • Martin has rental income from his apartment that will continue after he retires. He expects the growth rate to be lower than his expenses inflation
  • Covering his current living expenses like food, transport, bills, and insurance. All these expenses have their inflation rate
  • He also plans for future (old age high medical bills) that may not be covered by insurance, and health-related expenses, with higher inflation rate than normal living expenses
  • Dreams of travelling the world and travel fewer afterwards and stop travelling upon 80
  • Paying off his housing loan until 60
  • Upgrading his insurance in a few years, and he expects to pay until 80
  • Changing his car every few years until a certain age
  • He expects some expenses to decrease as he ages

Important Notes

  • All expense amounts are in today's values - the app will calculate future amounts based on inflation
  • Income amounts are future projections based on estimates and agreements
  • Some scenarios are for demonstration and may not apply to your situation

Martin wants to validate if his retirement plan is sustainable with:

  • His ongoing contributions during working years
  • Rental income throughout retirement
  • Various expenses at different life stages
  • Investment risk management strategies
