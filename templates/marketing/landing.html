Traditional Planning Tools Fall Short

Old Way

  • Assumes constant withdrawal throughout retirement
  • Uses single inflation rate for all expenses
  • Oversimplified investment assumptions with flat return
  • Calculate yourself how much you will have upon retirement
  • Missing risk handling with what-if
  • Missing important consideration for FIRE planning

DynaRetire Way

  • Adapts to changing expenses at different life stages
  • Different inflation rates for different expense types
  • Real history market testing with bucket strategies
  • Help you to project with contributions, returns, & growth
  • Switch to Minimum Spending, Option of Pause Balancing
  • Cater for pension fund withdrawal constraints & impact
