Our Mission

DynaRetire was created as a contribution to the retirement planning community, built by someone who experienced firsthand the challenges of planning for financial independence.

A Tool, Not a Replacement

While DynaRetire offers powerful simulations, we recognize the value of professional financial advisors who provide personalized guidance, tax expertise, and emotional support during financial decisions. This tool complements, rather than replaces, professional advice.

Community-Minded

We believe financial planning tools should be accessible to everyone. DynaRetire exists to help people understand the dynamics of retirement planning through interactive modeling, regardless of their wealth level or financial literacy.

The simulations provided are educational tools to help with planning decisions. For comprehensive financial advice tailored to your specific situation, we encourage consulting with a qualified financial professional.

Why Traditional Tools Aren't Enough

Old Way

  • Assumes constant withdrawal throughout retirement
  • Uses single inflation rate for all expenses
  • Calculate yourself how much you will have upon retirement
  • Oversimplified investment assumptions with flat return
  • Missing risk handling as part of the plan
  • Missing important consideration for FIRE planning

DynaRetire Way

  • Adapts to changing expenses at different life stages
  • Different inflation rates for different expense types
  • Help you to project with contributions, returns, & growth
  • Real history market testing*
  • Switch to Minimum Spending, Option of Pause Balancing
  • Cater for pension fund withdrawal constraints

* Why We Use Historical Testing Instead of Monte Carlo

Market crashes are a historical pattern. When one hits early in retirement, your financial plans may suffer. Unlike random simulations, historical testing can provide insight and simulate how they could impact your retirement cash flow, so you can have a plan B or make adjustments if needed.
